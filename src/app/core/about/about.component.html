About Us

With BookCompany, it’s easy to store the right book or song for every moment – on your personal collection. There are countless things you can upload - real or fiction - to your storage. So whether you’re behind the wheel, working out, partying or relaxing, the right book/music/film is always at your fingertips. Choose what you want to store, or let BookCompany surprise you. You can also browse through landmarks via the GoogleMap API integrated with the app. Also, the integrated New York Times API allows for current list of all best sellers of a specific genre/category in addition to displaying all the New York Times book reviews of any author of your choosing.

Browse your home feed. Your home feed is where you'll find your uploaded projects. We also provide directions on how to get to your nearest library, museum, festival, movie-screening, etc. Thanks to the Google Map Search and Places APIs, you can also obtain estimated time of arrival as well as accurate travel routes.

With the New York Times Best Sellers List and Reviews APIs, you can explore the details about your favorite author's books. In addition, articles (from categories like 'books') from the New York Times are also displayed in realtime thanks to the New York Times Articles API.
